Privacy Policy - Priori DB

Last Updated: 10/03/2025

Priori DB respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application. By using Priori DB, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Priori DB is designed to assist you in managing databases efficiently. To provide its functionality, the application may access, process, and store the following types of data locally on your device:

  • Database Connection Details:

    • Information required to connect to your databases.

  • User Interaction History:

    • Logs of queries executed and other interactions with the application.

    • This data is stored locally to help you track your activity and improve your workflow.

  • Configuration Data:

    • Local settings and preferences configured by you within the application.

Important: All data collected and stored by Priori DB remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

2. Access to Database Metadata

Priori DB allows users to connect to databases, execute queries and manage data base infraestructure . During this process, the application will retrieve metadata from the connected database (e.g., table structures, schema information) to display relevant details and assist with database management tasks. Priori DB does not transmit this data externally.

  • Local Processing: This metadata is processed locally on your device and is never sent to any third-party servers or servers associated with Priori DB.
  • Network Communication: All network communication occurs exclusively between your device and the database you are connected to. Priori DB will not send any data to third-party servers.

3. Internet Connectivity

Priori DB operates as an offline application and does not require internet connectivity for its functionality.

  • No Online Services: The application does not connect to the internet for updates, documentation access, or any other online services.
  • User-Initiated Connections: The only network interactions occur when you, the user, connect to an external database. These connections are entirely under your control.

4. Data Sharing

Priori DB does not share any user information with third parties.

  • Direct Database Interaction: All database interactions occur directly between your device and the configured database. Priori DB does not interfere with or access this data beyond what is necessary to provide its functionality.
  • No Third-Party Involvement: No data is transmitted to servers associated with Priori DB or any external parties.

5. Permissions

To function properly, Priori DB may require certain permissions on your device, such as access to local files or network connections.

  • Purpose of Permissions: These permissions are used solely to enable the application's functionality, such as connecting to databases, storing connections configurations, user settings, and saving interaction history locally.
  • No External Data Collection: The application does not use these permissions to collect, store, or transmit any data externally.

6. Security of Your Data

We take the security of your data seriously. All data is stored exclusively on your device and is not accessible to external parties.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

If any changes are made, the updated version will be made available on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.
